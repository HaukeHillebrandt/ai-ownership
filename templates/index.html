Diversifying AI Ownership

Hauke Hillebrandt

Ownership of advanced AI is concentrating fast — in a handful of firms, and in one or two countries. This paper argues that broadly distributed ownership would decentralize power, share the gains, and hedge against technological unemployment, and proposes three concrete policies to get there. It is cited in Nick Bostrom’s Open Global Investment model of AGI governance.
